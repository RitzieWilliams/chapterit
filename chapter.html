Finding My Rhythm

Oct 5, 2024

First game of the season and I felt way more composed than last year. The work we put in during preseason paid off — I wasn't rushing my reads or forcing shots in traffic. This is the standard I want to hold myself to.

Learning From the Loss

Oct 12, 2024

Tough road game. We got outworked on the boards in the second half and it cost us. I need to be more vocal on defensive rotations — that's on me as much as anyone.

Comeback Mindset

Oct 19, 2024

Down six at halftime and nobody panicked. Coach kept the message simple: defend and move the ball. We executed the game plan in the third quarter and it opened everything up.
